Smart Clean

Privacy Policy

Smart Clean is designed to help users manage storage locally on their devices while minimizing privacy risk.

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Who We Are

Smart Clean, also referred to in this policy as “we”, “us”, or “our”, provides local storage cleanup features such as media scanning, duplicate detection, file cleanup, and optional SMS or contact cleanup features.

2. Information We Access

Smart Clean primarily processes data locally on your device. Depending on the features you choose to use, the app may access:

  • Photos and videos, to identify large, duplicate, or similar media files
  • Files and folders on device storage, to analyze storage usage and cleanup candidates
  • SMS messages, if you enable SMS cleanup features
  • Contacts, if you enable contact cleanup or contact-related messaging features
  • App and purchase status information, to unlock Premium features after a Google Play purchase

3. How We Use Information

Any accessed information is used only to provide app functionality, including:

  • Analyzing storage usage
  • Finding duplicate, similar, outdated, or unnecessary files
  • Showing cleanup candidates and user-selected actions
  • Enabling optional SMS and contact cleanup features
  • Restoring and validating Premium purchase state through Google Play Billing

4. Local Processing

Our core storage and media analysis is intended to run locally on your device. We do not operate a cloud backend for storage scanning or media analysis.

Data processed for cleanup, duplicate detection, and similar analysis stays on your device unless required by a third-party service you explicitly use, such as Google Play Billing or Google AdMob.

5. Permissions

Smart Clean may request permissions including:

  • Photos / Media to scan images, videos, and related media files
  • Storage access to analyze storage usage and cleanup candidates
  • SMS for optional SMS cleanup features
  • Contacts for optional contact cleanup features

Permissions are requested only when relevant to the feature you are using. You may revoke permissions at any time in Android system settings.

6. Third-Party Services

Smart Clean may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing to process and restore in-app purchases
  • Google AdMob to display ads in the free version of the app

These services may process certain technical or account-related information under their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not provide a cloud service that uploads your storage, photos, SMS, or contacts for analysis.

Data may still be handled by Google services that you choose to interact with through the app, such as billing and ads, in accordance with Google’s policies.

8. Data Retention

Because Smart Clean primarily works with local data on your device, retention is largely controlled by you. Files, SMS messages, or contacts are only removed when you explicitly choose to delete them.

9. Children’s Privacy

Smart Clean is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.
